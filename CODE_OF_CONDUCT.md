# Contributor Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and technically constructive
project.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and assume good faith.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Share reproducible details while removing credentials and personal data.
- Respect licenses, creators, dataset subjects, and project rules.
- Accept maintainer decisions about safety, scope, and support capacity.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, sexualized conduct, deliberate
disruption, credential disclosure, or encouraging unlawful or abusive use is
not permitted.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove content, close participation, or report severe
conduct to the relevant platform. Report conduct privately through the
repository's security/contact channel rather than opening a public issue that
exposes personal information.
